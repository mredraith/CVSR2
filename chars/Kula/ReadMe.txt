---------------<Character Info>------------------

(**Note: 1-10 Ranking System: 1 = Lowest, 10 = Best)

 - Intelligence: 7
 - Strength: 4
 - Speed: 7
 - Stamina: 4
 - Energy Projection: 5
 - Fighting Ability: 7

While not particularly strong, Kula's true strength lies within her juggling potential.
Her speed is nice as well. She also has a few nice mixups at her disposal along with very useful command normals.
The key to victory will be to learn and utilize her juggling potential at it's fullest.

----------------<Move Details>-------------------

 - On counter hit One Inch wall bounces p2, except when command canceled, along with reseting the juggle points
 - Slider Shoot hits low
 - (EX)Diamond Breath can not be air guarded
 - Crow Bite has invulnerability on startup
 - EX Crow Bite has invulnerability up to the apex of her jump
 - (EX)Counter Shell destroys projectiles along with Kula will kick out her own projectile to counter
 - (EX)Ray Spin "Sit" follow up attack resets the juggle points, allowing you to follow up with any attack
 - EX Ray Spin resists normal/special projectiles, though not the follow up attacks
 - EX Diamond Shoot hits twice and knocks foes down
 - All of Kula's supers can not be air guarded
 - The block of ice for Glacier Shoot can be used as a shield on startup, it also can not be destroyed unless Kula- 
   herself is hit on startup.  
   
---------------<Gameplay Notes>------------------

COMBO SYSTEM:
 - Most Normal standing/crouching attacks can be canceled into Special and Super moves
 - Some Normal attacks can be command canceled into One Inch/Slider Shoot/Critical Ice 
 - Some Special moves can be canceled into Super moves
 - Performing a Super/MAX Cancel resets the juggle points but also reduces damage dealt
   
MAX MODE:
 - Your attack and defense is increased while in MAX Mode
 - EX moves can be performed at no power cost, though take away from time sustained in MAX mode
 - All Lv1 supers can be performed at no power cost, though immediately uses up all time in MAX mode
 - All Lv2 and higher supers will cost 1 power bar, along with using up all time in MAX mode
 - Even without a power bar, as long as you have time left in MAX mode, you can perform Lv1 supers/EX moves
 - You can not Power Charge/gain power while in MAX mode
 - You can not perform a Alpha Counter/Counter Movement while in MAX Mode
