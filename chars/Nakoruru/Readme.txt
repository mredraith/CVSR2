- Customized version of SNK Playmore's Nakoruru character, for Mugen 1.0



=====<Features>=====

- All the essential stuff
- Details and moves taken from her various video-game appearances and storyline
- Gameplay mixed from several Capcom games, including CvS2, SFZ3 and SF3
- Original and edited effects from various games
- Sounds from various games, including CvS2 and NBC
- Voice samples mainly from CvS2, but including NBC and SS as well
- Various intros, win poses and special animations
- Special intros versus Rera, Morrigan, and  "megalomaniac evil guys"
- Midnight Bliss special animation
- A.I.



=====<Movelist>=====

U - up          x - weak punch         a - weak kick
D - down        y - medium punch       b - medium kick
F - forward     z - strong punch       c - strong kick
B - back        p - any punch          k - any kick
s - start       2p- two punches        2k- two kicks

(Air)     - Move must be performed in the air.
(Air also)- Move can be performed either on the ground or in the air.
(EX)      - Move has an EX version, performed by pressing two punch/kick buttons.
(MAX) - Use two punch/kick buttons when performing a Super move to power it up.

- Elerush Kamui Rimse can be done from Mamahaha Ni Tsukamaru
- Elerush Kamui Rimse will only connect properly on a standing or crouching opponent
- Nakoruru can juggle after Irusuka Yatoro Lise with any attack
- Since Shirikoro Kamui Nomi heals Nakoruru, it can't be done if her life bar is full
- Jiketsu forfeits a round in favour of a full power bar
