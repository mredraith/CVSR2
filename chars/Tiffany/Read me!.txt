-Gameplay System>-------------------------------
- Some Normal attacks can be canceled into Special and Super moves.
- Some Special moves can be canceled into Super moves.
- Air Throw can be canceled into Special and Super Moves.
- MAX Groovy Special can be canceled into Air Throw.
- You can cancel from EX Groovy Knuckle into EX Beautiful Spin
- EX Exciting Kick can be canceled into some moves(Basic/Normal/Special/Super).
