=====<Move Details>=====

 - Death Lake Drive can catch foes in their hit states and isn't affected by the juggle system
 - Stomping is an overhead, except when command canceled
 - Death Lake Drive (Followup) can only be done from Medium or Heavy Gatling Attack
 - EX Gatling Attack can resist normal/special projectiles and resets the juggle points
 - (EX)Super Argentine Backbreaker, (EX)Napalm Stretch, (EX)Frankensteiner, (MAX)Ultra Argentine Backbreaker, Running Three, and Napalm Strike are command grabs
 - Light and Medium Super Argentine Backbreaker has auto-guard during it's starting frames
 - EX Super Argentine Backbreaker ground bounces, allowing you to follow up with attacks
 - The followups of Mount Tackle can be teched:
     .Clark Lift:	  		x
     .Reverse D.D.T:  			y/z
     .Rolling Cradle: 			k
 - (EX)Shining Wizard is unblockable but only hits crouching opponents
 - EX Shining Wizard goes into Frankensteiner and can resist normal/special projectiles

=====<Gameplay Notes>=====

COMBO SYSTEM:
 - Some Normal attacks can be canceled into Special and Super moves
 - Some Normal attacks can be command canceled into Step/Stomping
 - Some Special moves can be canceled into Super moves
 - Cancelling a Special, Super move or Custom Combo into a Super move resets the juggle points
   but also reduces its damage

MAX MODE:
 - Your attack and defense is increased while in MAX Mode
 - EX moves can be performed at no power cost, though take away from time sustained in MAX mode
 - All Lv1 supers can be performed at no power cost, though immediately uses up all time in MAX mode
 - All Lv2 and higher supers will cost 1 power bar, along with using up all time in MAX mode
 - Even without a power bar, as long as you have time left in MAX mode, you can perform Lv1 supers/EX moves
 - You can not Power Charge/gain power while in MAX mode
 - You can not perform a Alpha Counter while in MAX Mode
