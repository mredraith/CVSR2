Esaka
+ (hold to charge)


Drunken boxing counter move. Charge up to three levels. At level 2 she flashes white and the attack aquires a Special Property. If the attack is charged to full time it becomes unblockable. If the move hits the opponent while they are performing an attack, it aquires a Special Property. Esaka can be canceled into a quick dash by pressing , or into a back hop by pressing , up to and including when the move hits.

While charging, Xiangfei is able to sustain a single hit without taking damage. Note that everytime she absorbs a hit, her defense is lowered for a short time. Each additional hit she absorbs will accumulate the defense decreases.


Normal - Strikes out in front of her. Special Property: Temporarily stuns and crumples the enemy.



Hold - Strikes into the air. Special Property: Sends the opponent into a spin.



Hold - Hits low. Special Property: Knocks the opponent off their feet.



EX (Power Level 1) - Certain moves can be interrupted by performing Esaka. Requires 1 power bar.

Misc.

Mugen Version

In the main folder are three different folders with .def files corresponding to different Mugen versions. Copy and paste the approriate .def into the main folder to have the character behave properly to whichever version you're using.

NOTE: The character is set to Mugen 1.0 by default.
