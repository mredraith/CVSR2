----------------<Move Details>-------------------

- Rainbow Drop can catch foes in their hit states and isn't affected by the juggle system
- (EX)Ame No Murakumo can otg
- EX Ame No Murakumo can resist normal/special projectiles
- (EX)Formation A helpers have different properties, dependant on the type summoned:
   *Drone: Travels the slowest, can only be blocked low
   *Tiger: Travels the fastest, counts as an overhead
   *Eagle: Travels somewhere in between, can not be air guarded
- (EX)Formation A resets the juggle points
- EX Gram High/Air wall bounces p2
- EX Gram Low must be guarded low
- EX Vajra ground bounces p2 and resets the juggle points
- Hiryu does not need a active satellite to perform EX Formation B
- EX Formation B can absorb a hit
- Legion can not be air guarded
- While Ouroboros is active you can NOT gain power, activate Max Mode, or perform a Zero Counter

---------------<Gameplay Notes>------------------

COMBO SYSTEM:
 - Most Normal standing/crouching attacks can be canceled into Special and Super moves
 - Some Special moves can be canceled into Super moves
 - Performing a Super/MAX Cancel resets the juggle points but also reduces damage dealt
   
MAX MODE:
 - Your attack and defense is increased while in MAX Mode
 - EX moves can be performed at no power cost, though take away from time sustained in MAX mode
 - All Lv1 supers can be performed at no power cost, though immediately uses up all time in MAX mode
 - All Lv2 and higher supers will cost 1 power bar, along with using up all time in MAX mode
 - Even without a power bar, as long as you have time left in MAX mode, you can perform Lv1 supers/EX moves
 - You can not Power Charge/gain power while in MAX mode
 - You can not perform a Zero Counter while in MAX Mode
